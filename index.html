Devstork Platform for Development

Africa’s first remote first consulting platform that connects development consultants to donor-funded projects. On the DPD platform well trained consultants can sign up, bid for projects, get onboarded on project teams and get paid for executing projects using their spare time.

As Dervestork , we cater for you

Whether you are a consultant seeking to work on fast paced, exciting and paid consultancy gigs or an affiliate providing us leads to potential projects, our approach is a transparent, hands on, immersive and results oriented one. We emphasize technical rigour, rapid iterations, use of data to inform decisions, and quality.

  • Consultants on our platform are categorized into several teams led by DPD Project leads.
  • On projects, consultants can view their dashboards, collaborate on projects, view tasks, submit deliverables and request payments.
  • Consultants receive star ratings for gigs conducted. They can also refer other consultants and receive a reward if the referred consultant meets our standard
  • Our network of affiliates function as lead generators by scraping the web and other sources for potential grant applications that fit our capability areas.
  • Our affiliates are able to sign up to their dashboard, submit links to leads, view our actions on potential leads and receive feedback.
